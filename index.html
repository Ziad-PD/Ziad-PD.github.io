About Me

Hi, I'm Ziad — a Product Designer with a passion for turning complex problems into elegant, intuitive solutions. With a background in Business Information Systems and 2+ years of hands-on experience, I bridge the gap between user needs and business goals.

My work spans ERP systems, FinTech platforms, EdTech products, and enterprise solutions — always with a focus on clarity, scalability, and impact. I believe great design isn't just about aesthetics; it's about creating experiences that feel effortless.

When I step away from the screen, I'm exploring emerging design trends, mentoring aspiring designers, and contributing to the broader design community.
